Beond Treatment is Healing & Wellbeing

A Radical New Path to Healing Trauma & Ending Addiction

Beond helps heal deep traumas, uncovering the true sources of suffering, and identify root causes of cravings that enable harmful behaviors used to soothe long lasting pain.

Our approach rapidly reveals the lost or neglected authentic self beyond the darkest perceptions and concepts. Your experience at Beond will help identify and release what is on the other side of chemical dependency, long term addictions, and painful experiences of post-traumatic stress.

Heal Deeply, Safely, and with Longevity

Trauma is a wound that leaves many of us vulnerable to addiction and prone to recurring compulsion. Under the compassionate care of our team of highly experienced medical doctors, addiction specialists, and licensed psychotherapists, Beond Therapies safely facilitate accelerated self awareness and radical processing of core traumas at the root of depression, anxiety, substance use and lasting symptoms of PTSD.

Let go of Harmful Ways of Coping

To survive trauma we all create complex protection systems that help us survive. Many harmful behaviors like substance use, chronic isolation, deep depression, and anxiety develop as maladaptive coping responses to our unaddressed suffering. While these experiences may temporarily relieve pains from our past, they compound the harm we do to ourselves and those closest to us.

Beond Therapies are radically innovative. Designed to heal the deepest emotional wounds that underlie our negative responses, our treatments interupt addiction—stopping substance dependency, as well as patterns of non-substance related abusive behaviors such as gambling, eating disorders, overeating, and sexual compulsions.

learn more

Become your Authentic Self

No one chooses to be an addict or live a violent life. All of us are born deeply open-hearted: calm, connected, curious, and compassionate to ourselves and those around us. But the effects of trauma (large and small) can cause us to close ourselves off from our true self and others.

Deeply meaningful psycho-spiritual healing experiences at Beond can close the gap between our head and our heart, motivating us to move forward with new possibilities and discover our deepest sense of purpose, creating a new connection to our authentic self.
